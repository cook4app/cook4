Main Cook4 sections

Buy!

In this section, you'll find the dishes prepared by other users.

By default, dishes are ordered according to their proximity to you, which is based on their date/time and location.

By clicking the magnifying lens icon on top-right, you'll be able to filter the dishes based on: location, name, tags (e.g., "Dessert", "Chinese" or "vegan") and date/time.

By cliking on any dish, you'll be able to check it in detail, buy it or propose to the cook a swap with a dish of yours.

 

Sell!

In this section, you can put your own dishes on sale.

The first step is to create the dish. You can do so via the "Add a dish!" button, adding the information needed and one or more pictures. It's very important to add appropriate tags to your dish, since they'll ease its identification by users when they search.

Subsequently, you can put your dish on sale. You can do so right after creating it, or anytime after that, since dishes you create are automatically stored in the section "Registered dishes". Selling a dish requires to input the following information

  • Minimum data:
    • Delivery and/or collection transportation mode
    • Dish price for each transportation mode
    • Date and time of dish availability
    • Reference address (either for the buyer to collect the dish or, if you as the cook want to deliver it, for C4 to compute the user's distance and block users too far)
  • Optional data (you can enter them by clicking the downward arrow on the right)
    • Preparation time, in case you as the cook want to prepare the dish only if purchased by others. For example, setting the minimum notice time as 24h, the dish will disappear from section "Buy" 24h before the date/time in which it will be available, so you'll avoid cooking it unless anyone has bought it by then
    • Maximum distance that you as the cook are willing to cover, if you have selected the option of yourself delivering the dish to the buyer
    • Number of dish portions
    • Frequency of the offer, that is, whether it's a one-off or a repeat one, in which case you'll be asked to enter the date range and frequency

Once your dish is on sale, it will be visible for all other users in the section "Buy!". It will then disappear when all its portions have been bought or after its date and time (minus the advance notice time, if you set any).

Whether or not on sale, all the dishes you've created will remain in the section "Sell!", under "Registered dishes". This way, all ratings your dishes receive will stick with them and as you put them on sale again they'll be visible. You can nonetheless remove any dish from this list at any point in time.

 

Swap

In this section, you'll find the swap proposals that you made or received but haven't been accepted or rejected yet. It is therefore possible, from this section, to accept or reject the swap proposals received and check the status of the ones you made to other users.

When a swap proposal is accepted by the receiver, C4 removes it form this section and originates two transactions for each of the two users. These will be immediately visible in the section "Transactions". When a swap proposal is rejected by the receiver or canceled by the proposer before any response, C4 simply removes it from this section and originates no other transactions.

 

Transactions

In this section, you can see all the dishes that you ordered from other users ("To receive") or the ones that other users ordered from you ("To prepare"). Every time a user buys a dish from you, you'll receive a notification on your phone and the transaction will automatically appear in this section.

By clicking on any transaction, the person who will phisically go to collect or deliver the dish will see the full details of the exchange address. A link to Google Maps will also show the directions to reach there.

As a transaction is completed, each user will see, in this section and in relation to that transaction, a message inviting them to rate the coutnerpart. As soon as the vote is given, the transaction will disappear from this section.

Finally, from this section it is also possible to cancel a pending transaction. However, this automatically triggers a negative score for the user, which everyone else will see and may therefore prevet the user from using Cook4 in the future.

 

 

Evaluating dishes and users

Both cooks and buyers, after each transaction, evaluate the counterpart from the section "Transactions". Evaluations are on a scale from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest) and cover:

  • Service quality (given by both users), concerning the quality of the interaction with the other user. This includes, for example, punctuality, courtesy, availability and reliability
  • Dish quality (given only by the buyer to the cook), concerning the quality of the food

Based on these reciprocal evaluations, when a users explores the dishes on offer and their cooks, s/he can check four aspects:

  • Dish rating, shown next to the dish name
  • User's overall evaluation (e.g., "Beginner" or "Chef", plus related score), based on the combination of service and dish quality ratings received
  • Cook evaluation (only score), based only on the dish quality ratings received
  • Proximity index (a number in a small golden circle next to the cook's name), which automatically identifies "reliable" users. Number 1 indicates users that you yourself previously rated 4 or 5, while number 2 indicates cooks that were previously ranked 4 or 5 not by you, but by other users whom you voted 4 or 5 

 

 

Communications

With C4, it is possible to easily speak with other users, as well as with our team.

To write to other users, you can use the chat icon (a comics bubble) from the page of any dish on sale. This way, you can ask the cook for additional information on his/her dish, or for a possible change in the delivry time.

To write to us, in addition to our e-mail address (hello@cook4app.com), you can use the "Report" button placed next to each dish and user profile page. This botton, a bell-shaped icon, allows you to notify us of any content or behaviour you consider inappropriate. This will allow us to investigate at the earliest and take any action needed.

 

 

General functions

From the top-left menu, you can at any time access the general functions of C4, in order to:

  • View and edit your profile, including name, picture and introductory claim
  • View the list of C4 cooks and their profile pages
  • Retrieve the open chat conversations with other users
